Only those who dare to fail greatly can achieve great things. Don't be afraid to fail. Don't waste energy trying to hide failure. Learn from your failures and move on to the next challenge.

To succeed in a coding interview, consider the following points :

👉Ensure you have a strong understanding of data structures (e.g., arrays, linked lists, trees) and algorithms (e.g., searching, sorting).

👉 Practice improves problem-solving skills and builds confidence. Use on platforms like LeetCode, HackerRank, or others.

👉 Understand the time and space complexity of your code. Interviewers often evaluate efficiency.

👉Clearly communicate your thought process. Interviewers often value problem-solving approach over a perfect solution.

👉Ask questions to clarify doubts before diving into the solution.

👉 Revisit your previous coding projects and understand the decisions you made.

👉 Learning from errors is crucial for improvement so when you make a mistake, understand why it happened.

👉Practice coding interviews with friends, or use platforms that offer mock interviews.

👉Consider edge cases in your solution. This shows a deeper understanding of the problem.

👉Take a moment to think before answering, and don't be afraid to ask for clarification if needed. Manage stress during the interview is importa👌

👉Be prepared to discuss your past projects and experiences. Interviewers might ask about your role in previous jobs or projects.

👉Send a thank-you email after the interview. This can leave a positive impression on the interviewer.
